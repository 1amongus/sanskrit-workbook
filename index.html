Welcome to the Samskrita Swadhyaya workbook for Sanksepa Ramayanam (Summary of Ramayana) from the Third Level (तृतीया दीक्षा) course by Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan.

This workbook contains 74 exercises (अभ्यासाः) covering all 100 shlokas of the Sanksepa Ramayanam. Select an exercise from the sidebar to begin.

Each exercise includes fill-in-the-blanks, sandhi exercises, word analysis, sentence construction, and more.
